Using javatuples with Maven

In order to include javatuples in your maven-managed project, you will have to declare your dependency like this:

    <dependency>
        <groupId>org.javatuples</groupId>
        <artifactId>javatuples</artifactId>
        <version>[version]</version>
        <scope>compile</scope>
    </dependency>
